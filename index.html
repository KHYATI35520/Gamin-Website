Dragon Escape

Dragon Escape is an arcade game where you control your character that has to jump through many obstacles that is the dragon.In order to score high you need to escape from Dragon's reach,as soon as you collide with the dragon the game gets over.It is very easy to play,just you need forward,backward arrows for the movement of your bird and press spacebar to jump over the Dragon.It's a stress booster,relaxing and very enjoyable game!

Hungry Snake

Get your thinking caps ready because we're gonna take this snake game in a new level. Snake and Blocks is a new take on the classic game snakes. In this game, your mission is to go as far as you can. You need to collect all those boxes which will add up in your snake's length. You need your snake to be longer so you can go through the blocks. The number of boxes you attache to the body of your snake that will be your score.Higher the boxes,higher will be your score. It's highly addictive, enjoyable and mind enhancing!
